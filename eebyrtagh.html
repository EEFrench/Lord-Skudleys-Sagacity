the Eebyrtagh

This ship, the Eebyrtagh, took me six months to build, from concept drawings to finished product. She’s made of poster board and foam core, with strips of paper to form the planks. the flag I drew in PowerPoint then just printed and mounted. The figurehead is a dire lion and a verok.

I made her for our 7th Sea role playing game. The Eebyrtagh has five decks, the hold, gun-deck with the surgery, main deck which has officer’s/passenger’s quarters, galley and kitchen (under the forecastle), top deck and captain’s/admiral’s cabin and forecastle deck and finally quarter deck.

She stands 28 inches at the Main Mast, 10 inches to the Quarter Deck, 8 inches to the Top Deck, 5 inches to the main deck and is 33 inches from stem to stern..
